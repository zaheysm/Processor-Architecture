Question

When connecting external ROM memory, which control signals are used?

Answer

Of the listed control signals, only ECLK is used. R/*W is not needed for a ROM, since it is only read. *LSTRB and ADDR0 are not needed for control since it does no harm to read a word even when only a byte is requested. The high-order address lines are used for address decoding and could be considered control signals, but are not considered so by the question instructions.
