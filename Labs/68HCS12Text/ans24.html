Question

Assuming a 25 MHz system clock using the PLL and a 5 MHz crystal, what pulse width modulator settings will create a period of 5.4 microseconds with a positive going pulse width of 2.4 microseconds on channel 2?

Answer

Based on the 25 MHz system clock, without using the prescaler or divider we get a 40 ns clock period for Clock B. The maximum setting of PWMPER2, 255, would give a period of 10.2 uS, so there is sufficient range for this problem. Register settings would be:
PWME = $04 (PWME2=1)
PWMPOL = $04 (PPOL2=1)
PWMPER2 = 135 (5.4/.04)
PWMDTY2 = 60 (2.4/.04)
with other PWM registers being 0.
