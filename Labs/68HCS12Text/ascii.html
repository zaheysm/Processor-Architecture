ASCII Character Chart

 x    0x  1x   2x  3x  4x  5x  6x  7x
 0    NUL DLE      0   @   P   `   p  
 1    SOH DC1  !   1   A   Q   a   q  
 2    STX DC2  "   2   B   R   b   r  
 3    ETX DC3  #   3   C   S   c   s  
 4    EOT DC4  $   4   D   T   d   t  
 5    ENQ NAK  %   5   E   U   e   u  
 6    ACK SYN  &   6   F   V   f   v  
 7    BEL ETB  '   7   G   W   g   w  
 8    BS  CAN  (   8   H   X   h   x  
 9    HT  EM   )   9   I   Y   i   y  
 a    LF  SUB  *   :   J   Z   j   z  
 b    VT  ESC  +   ;   K   [   k   {  
 c    FF  FS   ,   <   L   \   l   |  
 d    CR  GS   -   =   M   ]   m   }  
 e    SO  RS   .   >   N   ^   n   ~  
 f    SI  US   /   ?   O   _   o   DEL

Notes: Codes 0 to $1f and $7f are non-printing, but represent special functions. The most commonly used functions are: CR - carriage return, LF - line feed, HT - horizontal tab, BS - backspace, BEL - bell, NUL - null (ignored), ESC - escape. Code $20 prints an empty space.
