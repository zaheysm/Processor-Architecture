Question

What sequence of instructions will generate a positive pulse on port J pin 0, assuming the pin is initially configured for output and is in the low state. Assuming a 24 MHz system clock, what will be the width of the pulse?

Answer

    bset ptj #$1
    bclr ptj #$1

Since the port is written at the same point in time in both of these instructions, the pulse width will be the time it takes to execute one instruction, 4 clock cycles, or 167 ns.
