Store the multiplicand value in word location $1000 (bytes $1000 and $1001), and the multiplier in word location $1002. Click on the digit and type the desired value to change. When the program runs, the product will be in locations $1004-$1007.

The Simulator will appear in a separate window. When this page is exited, the simulator will stop. The controls in the simulator for loading code, generating a log file, and taking a snapshot are disabled when run from the browser.
