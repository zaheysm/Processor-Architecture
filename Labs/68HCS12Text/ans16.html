Question

Look up the documentation for the Serial Peripheral Interface (SPI) I/O device module. List the register names and the register type they belong to.

Answer

The names of the registers usually say what they are. In this case the control registers are SPI0CR1, SPI0CR2, and SPI0BR (the baud rate register meets the definition of a control register). There is one status register, SPI0SR, and one data register, SPI0DR.
