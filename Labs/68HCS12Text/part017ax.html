View the Parallel port window for the trafic lights (row of LEDs) and the street sensor (right most switch is checked for car present, unchecked for no car present). Since this example uses the DBUG12 emulation, you will have to press Go twice.

The lights cycle ten times faster than designed to reduce the simulation time.

The Simulator will appear in a separate window. When this page is exited, the simulator will stop. The controls in the simulator for loading code, generating a log file, and taking a snapshot are disabled when run from the browser.
