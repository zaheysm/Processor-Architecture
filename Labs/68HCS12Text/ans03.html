Question

Convert the decimal values 31 and -22 to 8 bit two's complement binary, add them together, and convert the sum back to decimal. Show your work for each step of the problem.

Answer

31 is 00011111 in binary. 22 is 00010110, so -22 is 11101001 + 1, or 11101010. Added together we get 00001001, which is 9 decimal.
