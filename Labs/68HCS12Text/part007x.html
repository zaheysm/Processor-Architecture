You will need to press Go once for each of the three sample data sets. The program calculates I2=C1+I1 and I3=C2+I1, where C1 is an unsigned byte at $1000, C2 is a signed byte at $1001, and I1 through I3 are signed words at locations $1002, $1004, and $1006 respectively.

The Simulator will appear in a separate window. When this page is exited, the simulator will stop. The controls in the simulator for loading code, generating a log file, and taking a snapshot are disabled when run from the browser.
