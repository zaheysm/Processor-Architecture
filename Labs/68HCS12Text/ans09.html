Question

Accumulator A contains a value in the range 10 through 100. Can the value be considered signed? Can it be considered unsigned?

Answer

Since the range of signed values is -128 through 127 and 10 through 100 is within that range, it can be considered signed. Since the range of unsigned values is 0 through 255 and 10 through 100 is within that range, it can also be considered unsigned.
